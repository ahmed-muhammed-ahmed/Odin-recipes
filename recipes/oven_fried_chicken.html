Crispy Oven Fried Chicken

Oven Fried Chicken is a flavorful and healthier alternative to traditional deep-fried chicken. This recipe delivers the same satisfying crunch without the excess oil, making it a guilt-free indulgence. The chicken pieces are coated in a seasoned breading, then baked to perfection in the oven, resulting in a crispy and golden exterior while maintaining the juicy tenderness inside.

The secret to the success of this dish lies in the combination of herbs and spices that create a mouthwatering flavor profile. Whether you're looking for a quick weeknight dinner or planning a family gathering, Crispy Oven Fried Chicken is a crowd-pleaser that will have everyone reaching for seconds. Serve it with your favorite sides for a delicious and wholesome meal that satisfies your craving for fried chicken without the added grease.

Oven Fried Chicken Ingredients

Here's what you’ll need to make Oven Fried Chicken:

  • 12 chicken thighs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 3 eggs
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika

Steps To Make Oven Fried Chicken:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place flour in a shallow plate or bowl and season with salt and pepper to taste. Put bread crumbs in another shallow plate or bowl and beat eggs in another bowl.
  3. Dredge chicken piece by piece in the flour, then the egg, then the bread crumbs, until all pieces are coated.
  4. Pour oil into a large baking dish; add the breaded chicken thighs and sprinkle with paprika to taste.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, then turn pieces over and bake for another 30 minutes. Remove from the oven and drain on paper towels.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
